JUMP’ESTIVAL

This is a jumping competition which covers a wide range of different categories. This year it will take place in the prestigious location Pôle International de Deauville from the 25th to the 28th of July.

OPEN REGIONAL

Organized by the horseball pool of the association, the Open Regional is a horseball competition which was accessible last 7th of May for local teams.

Dressage des grandes ecoles (DGE)

Also called the DGE, it is the dressage competition available for students of French universities. However, this year it was cancelled due to some difficulties, but we are looking forward to seeing you there next year.
